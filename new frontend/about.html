What are Crop Diseases?

Crop diseases are caused by fungi, bacteria, viruses, and environmental stress. They reduce yield, damage quality, and threaten food security worldwide.

Types of Crop Diseases

🦠 Fungal Diseases (Rust, Blight)
🧬 Bacterial Diseases
🧫 Viral Infections
🌡 Environmental Stress

Global Impact

Crop diseases cause 30–40% global yield loss annually, costing billions of dollars and threatening food supply chains.

AI in Agriculture

AI can detect diseases instantly using image recognition, helping farmers take action before crops are damaged.
